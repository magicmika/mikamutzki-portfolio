## 2026-07-10 — Clean restart

- **Status:** APPROVED
- **Question:** Should Foundation V2 inherit the historical Figma system?
- **Decision:** Foundation V2 is a clean restart in a new Figma file. Historical tokens, components and layouts are not current authority.
- **Rationale:** Prevents accidental inheritance of unverified decisions and creates a credible design-system process.
- **Consequences:** Every reusable decision must be reviewed or recreated intentionally.

## 2026-07-10 — Creative hierarchy

- **Status:** APPROVED
- **Decision:** Swiss Design creates order and structure; Bauhaus provides plakative composition and stage; Neobrutalism provides selective statement.
- **Rationale:** Each influence receives a distinct job, avoiding stylistic collage.
- **Evidence:** Deep Research brief and report.
- **Revisit when:** Application tests show that one influence obstructs usability, storytelling or media evidence.

## 2026-07-10 — Typeface baseline

- **Status:** APPROVED
- **Decision:** Switzer is the only current typeface. A second family remains a separate future decision.
- **Rationale:** Distinction should first be tested through scale, weight, spacing, line breaks and rhythm.

## 2026-07-10 — Colour attitude

- **Status:** APPROVED AS ATTITUDE; VALUES OPEN
- **Decision:** Avoid muddy grey, taupe, greige, weakly desaturated and indecisive systems. Explore clear, saturated, graphic, controlled colour.
- **Open:** Palette, accent logic, semantic roles and accessible pairs.
- **Note:** Blue and orange used in tests are test colours, not approved brand colours.

## 2026-07-11 — Perception priority

- **Status:** APPROVED
- **Decision:** First read should communicate visual authorship, colour, layout and compositional energy. Second read should reveal precision and system competence.
- **Rationale:** Portfolio quality is often judged first through the total visual experience.
- **Consequence:** Signal Grid is not the lead territory; it remains a structural quality framework.

## 2026-07-11 — Contextual dominance

- **Status:** APPROVED PRINCIPLE
- **Decision:** The system may dominate first impression and transitional moments. It must not damage the legibility or evaluability of evidence.
- **Working formulation:** `The system leads the experience. The work leads the evidence.`
- **Rationale:** The portfolio itself is a design artifact and proof of Mika's authorship.

## 2026-07-11 — Territory roles

- **Status:** CANDIDATE LEAD DIRECTION
- **Decision:** Constructive Poster is the lead territory. Signal Grid provides structural precision. Hard Cut Editorial provides storytelling, rhythm and long-form discipline.
- **Rationale:** This supports a narrative sequence of attraction, orientation, depth, evidence, emphasis and resolution.
- **Validation required:** Home Hero, Floom Case Intro and Gallery Spread.
- **Not yet approved:** Concrete colours, grid, type scale, media rules, motion, tokens or components.

## 2026-07-11 — zeroheight role

- **Status:** APPROVED
- **Decision:** Use the 14-day Starter trial as a temporary documentation and case-study prototype. zeroheight is not part of the final tool stack or a source of truth.
- **Rationale:** It can demonstrate documentation maturity without creating long-term dependency.
- **Permanent record:** Markdown documents and Git history.
- **Visual evidence:** Figma.
- **Publication prototype:** zeroheight.
