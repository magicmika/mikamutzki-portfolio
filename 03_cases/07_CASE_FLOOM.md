## Project summary

Floom is a fictional mental wellbeing product concept developed as a capstone project during the Spiced Academy UX/UI program.

The product explores support flows and interface patterns for moments of stress and panic-like states. It includes structured guidance, simplified AI-assisted support, calming exercises, grounding or breathing interactions, and distraction-based features.

Floom must not be described as a launched product, a real client project, a medical product, a therapy product, or a clinically validated intervention.
